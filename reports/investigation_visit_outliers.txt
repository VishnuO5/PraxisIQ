Investigation: Statistical Visit Outlier Detection

Objective:
Identify patients with unusually high visit frequencies.

Method:

Mean Visits = 4.80

Standard Deviation = 3.38

Outlier Threshold = 11.56

Rule:

Patients with Total_Visits > 11.56
were flagged as outliers.

Results:

31 patients identified.

All flagged patients recorded exactly 13 visits.

Assessment:

The identical visit count across all outliers suggests a dataset generation ceiling rather than naturally occurring patient behavior.

Conclusion:

The statistical anomaly detector successfully identified visit-frequency outliers. Investigation suggests the pattern is likely caused by dataset design constraints.
