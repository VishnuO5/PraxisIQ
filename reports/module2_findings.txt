Module 2 - Statistical Analysis Findings

Objective:
Determine whether patient visit frequency differs across treatment types.

Method:
One-Way ANOVA

Results:

F Statistic: 5.3727
P Value: < 0.001

Conclusion:

A statistically significant difference exists between treatment groups.

Interpretation:

Patient visit frequency varies depending on treatment type, suggesting that some treatments require more patient engagement and follow-up visits than others.

Data Quality Notes:

Minor treatment naming inconsistencies were identified:

- Root Canal / root Canal
- Aligner / Aligners

These inconsistencies were documented and should be standardized in future analytical workflows.
