Investigation: Follow-Up Risk Queue

Objective:
Identify patients requiring follow-up who did not return.

Results:

High-Risk Patients Identified: 42

Key Pattern:

All high-risk patients recorded exactly one visit.

Observed Workflow:

Visit 1
→ Follow-Up Required
→ No Follow-Up Completed

Risk Treatments:

- Root Canal
- Implant
- Fixed Bridge
- Complete Denture
- Partial Denture

Assessment:

Patients requiring multiple-stage treatments demonstrated the highest follow-up risk.

Conclusion:

A risk queue of 42 patients was identified. These patients may require proactive outreach or intervention.
