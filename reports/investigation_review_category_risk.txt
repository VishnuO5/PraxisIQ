Investigation: Review Category Risk Analysis

Objective:
Identify the categories associated with the lowest review ratings.

Results:

Treatment      : 1.39
Pricing        : 2.03
Waiting Time   : 2.11
Staff          : 2.38
Communication  : 2.56
Neutral        : 3.00
Positive       : 4.78

Findings:

1. Treatment-related complaints have the lowest average rating and represent the highest-risk category.

2. Pricing and Waiting Time are secondary risk categories.

3. Communication and Staff issues show moderate dissatisfaction levels.

4. Positive reviews dominate overall volume but risk-focused analysis reveals specific problem areas.

Conclusion:

Treatment, Pricing, and Waiting Time should be prioritized for further investigation and corrective actions.
