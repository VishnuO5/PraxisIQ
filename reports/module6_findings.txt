Module 6 - Service Quality Analysis Findings

Objective:
Analyze customer feedback to identify key service quality issues and improvement opportunities.

Dataset:
300 Reviews

Findings:

1. Treatment Issues
   - Review Count: 36
   - Average Rating: 1.39
   - Lowest-rated category.

2. Pricing Issues
   - Review Count: 34
   - Average Rating: 2.03

3. Waiting Time Issues
   - Review Count: 36
   - Average Rating: 2.11

4. Communication Issues
   - Review Count: 39
   - Average Rating: 2.56

5. Staff Issues
   - Review Count: 29
   - Average Rating: 2.38

Overall Service Quality

Total Reviews:
300

Negative Review Percentage:
58.0%

Conclusion:

Treatment quality, pricing concerns, and waiting time are the most significant drivers of negative customer feedback.

Improvement efforts should prioritize treatment experience, cost transparency, and appointment efficiency.
